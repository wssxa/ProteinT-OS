# Leave & Attendance Handbook

Employees and long-term contractors accrue paid leave monthly based on contract terms and local policy.

## Annual Leave

- Full-time employees receive 15 days of annual leave each calendar year.
- Leave requests must be submitted at least 3 business days in advance in WeCom.
- Managers should approve or reject requests within 1 business day.

## Sick Leave

- Up to 10 paid sick leave days are available each year.
- A medical note is required for absences longer than 2 consecutive days.

## Carry-over

- Up to 5 unused annual leave days can be carried into Q1 of the following year.
- Remaining unused days after March 31 are forfeited unless an approved exception exists.

## Evidence and audit

HR exports approved leave records every Friday and stores them under policy artifacts.
Department heads review exceptions weekly.
